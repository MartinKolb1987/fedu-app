The Idea

The idea for fedu was born by us, three students of Interactive Media in Augbsurg, Germany.
Two of us (Martin and Mathias), started fedu as their Bachelor-Thesis in November 2012.
Many long weeks of programming and discussing and more programming, we launched fedu in July 2013.
